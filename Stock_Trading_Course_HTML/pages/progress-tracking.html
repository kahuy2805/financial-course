Chapter 0

Progress Tracking

Overview

This section provides tools and frameworks for monitoring your development throughout your trading education journey. Regular progress tracking keeps you motivated, accountable, and aware of what's working (and what isn't).

Time Commitment: 15-30 minutes weekly + monthly review

Why Track Progress?

  1. Motivation - Seeing progress reinforces your commitment
  2. Accountability - Weekly tracking keeps you on schedule
  3. Learning - Identifies patterns and areas for improvement
  4. Adjustment - Lets you pivot when something isn't working
  5. Celebration - Acknowledges milestones and wins
  6. Growth - Documents your transformation over time

How to Use These Tools

Weekly (15-20 minutes)

  1. Fill out Weekly Check-In Template
  2. Update Weekly Tracking Dashboard
  3. Complete 1-2 journal prompts
  4. Celebrate any wins

Monthly (30-45 minutes)

  1. Complete Monthly Review Template
  2. Update all tracking spreadsheets
  3. Take knowledge checkpoint quiz
  4. Update skill validation checklist
  5. Plan next month's focus

Quarterly (1-2 hours)

  1. Complete quarterly summary
  2. Review all monthly data
  3. Calculate performance metrics
  4. Identify patterns and trends
  5. Set goals for next quarter

Annually (2-3 hours)

  1. Review annual P/L
  2. Celebrate major milestones
  3. Assess overall progress
  4. Document lessons learned
  5. Set vision for next year

  • [ ] Paper: Notebook, journal, printed templates
