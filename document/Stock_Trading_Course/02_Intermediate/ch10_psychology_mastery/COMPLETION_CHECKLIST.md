## Common Completion Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Skipping exercises**
   - Exercises are where behavior change happens
   - Reading without practice = no change
   - Complete all 8 exercises fully

2. **Not implementing in actual trading**
   - Paper trading doesn't count
   - Systems only work when actually used
   - Implement immediately in real account

3. **Implementing too many systems at once**
   - Builds overwhelm instead of habit
   - Implement one per week
   - Master each before adding next

4. **Not tracking progress**
   - What you don't measure, you don't improve
   - Track daily discipline scores
   - Review weekly statistics
   - Monthly assessment required

5. **Inconsistent routine adherence**
   - Missing 2-3 days breaks the habit
   - 80%+ adherence minimum
   - Track visibly
   - Reset when missed

6. **Expecting instant results**
   - Psychology changes take 4-12 weeks minimum
   - Be patient with process
   - Trust the systems
   - Results follow consistency
