Recommended Next Steps

Based on your assessment results, you should:

If Overall Skill Level is BEGINNER:

  1. Start with Foundation Learning Path
  2. Focus heavily on Psychology section
  3. Begin with paper trading before live trading
  4. Work with a mentor or coach (highly recommended)
  5. Plan for 8-12 weeks minimum before considering live trading

If Overall Skill Level is EARLY INTERMEDIATE:

  1. Choose between Foundation Reinforcement or Intermediate Path
  2. Fill specific knowledge gaps identified
  3. Begin small live trading with 1-2% position sizing
  4. Focus on consistency and psychology
  5. Plan for 10-14 weeks to reach next level

If Overall Skill Level is INTERMEDIATE:

  1. Choose Advanced Path or Specialized Path
  2. Deep-dive into your specific interest area
  3. Increase position sizing gradually as consistency improves
  4. Develop a unique trading approach
  5. Consider mentoring others to deepen your understanding

If Overall Skill Level is ADVANCED:

  1. Choose Specialization Path
  2. Focus on optimization and high-conviction setups
  3. Explore options, multi-leg strategies, or other advanced topics
  4. Consider teaching/mentoring roles
  5. Work on business/capital management aspects

General Recommendations:

  • Psychology Score 1-2: Prioritize mindset work above technical learning
  • Psychology Score 3-4: Balance technical and psychological development
  • Psychology Score 4-5: Focus technical learning; maintain psychology foundations
