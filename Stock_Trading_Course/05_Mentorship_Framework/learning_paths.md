**Select ONE primary path below, then reference it throughout your learning journey.**

## PATH 1: FUNDAMENTALS

### For Whom
- Never traded before
- Want complete foundation before any live trading
- Need to understand markets from ground up
- Skill Level: 1.0-2.0/5.0

### Duration
8-10 weeks (10-15 hours/week)

### Overall Goal
Develop comprehensive understanding of stock markets, basic analysis, and safe trading practices before risking any real capital.

### Phase 1: Market Basics (Weeks 1-2)

**Concepts to Master:**
- How stock markets work
- Market structure and participants
- Order types and execution
- Brokerage accounts and platforms
- Trading vs. investing distinction

**Recommended Content:**
1. Course: 00_Course_Overview - Full course orientation
2. Course: 01_Foundation - Module 1: Market Fundamentals
3. Course: 01_Foundation - Module 2: Essential Concepts
4. Video: "Stock Market Basics" (Khan Academy or similar)
5. Reading: "The Intelligent Investor" - Introduction chapter

**Activities:**
- [ ] Open a paper trading account (no real money)
- [ ] Complete trading platform tutorial
- [ ] Practice 5 simulated trades
- [ ] Read one book chapter on market basics

**Checkpoint Quiz:**
- What are the major stock exchanges and their characteristics?
- How do market orders differ from limit orders?
- What are the main account types (cash, margin)?
- What fees and commissions should I expect?

**Success Metrics:**
- Understanding market mechanics (80%+ on checkpoint quiz)
- Comfort navigating trading platform
- Clear understanding of risks involved

## Pivoting Between Paths

**It's okay to switch paths if:**
- Original choice wasn't the right fit
- Your goals changed
- You discovered different interests
- Results suggest different approach is better

**How to Pivot:**
1. Acknowledge what you learned from original path
2. Clearly identify why you're switching
3. Review new path requirements
4. Don't lose progress—integrate what you've learned
5. Commit to new path for at least 4 weeks
