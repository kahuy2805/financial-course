# Prerequisites and Requirements

## What You Need Before Starting

This course is designed for complete beginners, but certain prerequisites will help you succeed. This page outlines what you need (and don't need) before diving in.

---

## ‚úÖ Required Prerequisites

### 1. Basic Math Skills
You should be comfortable with:
- Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division
- Percentages (e.g., calculating 2% of $10,000)
- Basic fractions and decimals
- Simple ratios

**Example:**
- If a stock costs $50 and you buy 10 shares, how much did you spend? ($500)
- What is 1% of $25,000? ($250)

**If you struggle with these**, review basic math before starting or keep a calculator handy.

### 2. Reading Comprehension
- Able to read and understand English at a high school level
- Comfortable with new vocabulary when definitions are provided
- Can follow written instructions and examples

### 3. Computer/Internet Access
- Basic computer skills (browsing websites, using software)
- Reliable internet connection
- Ability to open and view documents
- Email address for broker account setup (covered in Chapter 2)

### 4. Time Commitment
Minimum recommended:
- **5-10 hours per week** for casual learners
- **10-15 hours per week** for standard pace
- **20+ hours per week** for intensive study

Less time is possible but will extend completion timeline.

### 5. Realistic Mindset
- Understanding that trading involves risk of loss
- Willingness to learn and practice before using real money
- Patience to follow a structured learning path
- Openness to managing emotions and psychology

## üß† Mental Prerequisites

### Emotional Readiness
Ask yourself honestly:

**Can you handle losing money without emotional distress?**
- Trading involves losses
- Even the best traders lose regularly
- You must be emotionally prepared for this reality

**Are you willing to be patient?**
- Learning takes months, not days
- Profitability takes even longer
- Quick riches are a myth

**Can you follow rules and discipline?**
- Trading requires strict rule-following
- Emotional impulses must be controlled
- Discipline matters more than intelligence

**Are you honest with yourself?**
- You'll need to acknowledge mistakes
- Track both wins and losses accurately
- Admit when emotions affect decisions

**If you answered "no" to these**, work on mindset before trading with real money. The course includes extensive psychology training.

## üìû Questions Before Starting?

### "I don't have any trading experience. Is that okay?"
Perfect! This course is designed for complete beginners.

### "I'm not good at math. Can I still learn trading?"
Yes. We use basic math only and provide calculators for everything.

### "I don't have money to trade. Should I wait?"
No. Start with paper trading (free). Real money comes much later, if at all.

### "I'm older/younger. Does age matter?"
No. Trading is for any age. Concepts are universal.

### "English isn't my first language. Will I struggle?"
Possibly with some vocabulary, but we define all technical terms. Google Translate can help with anything unclear.

### "I have a full-time job. Can I still learn?"
Absolutely. Many successful traders work full-time. Part-time study works well.

### "I failed at trading before. Should I try again?"
Yes, if you approach it differently this time with structured education and proper risk management.
