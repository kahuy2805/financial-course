COMMON QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Do I need to own stocks to trade options?
A: No, but covered calls and protective puts do. Spreads don't.

Q: How much capital do I need?
A: For covered calls: $15,000-20,000. For spreads: $5,000-10,000.

Q: Can I make money trading options?
A: Yes, with education and discipline. Expect 0.5-1.5% monthly.

Q: What's the biggest mistake beginners make?
A: Not using stop losses and risking too much per trade.

Q: How long until I can trade for real money?
A: Minimum 3 months paper trading with discipline.

Q: Which strategy is best?
A: Covered calls for income, spreads for defined risk.

Q: What about earnings plays?
A: Advanced strategy; master basics first.

Q: Do I need advanced math?
A: No, just percentages and multiplication.

DISCLAIMERS & WARNINGS

EDUCATIONAL CONTENT:

- Examples are for illustration only

OPTIONS TRADING RISKS:
- Options can result in total loss of investment
- Leverage amplifies both gains and losses
- Complex instruments; may not be suitable for all
- Markets are unpredictable

BEFORE TRADING:
- Consult a financial advisor
- Paper trade for minimum 3 months
- Start with tiny position sizes
- Never risk capital you can't afford to lose
- Follow all local regulations

RESPONSIBLE TRADING:
- Only risk 1-2% per trade
- Use stop losses on every trade
- Diversify across strategies and sectors
- Monitor positions daily
- Review performance monthly
- Adjust strategy based on results
